ABOUT

What is Hack the Bay?



Hack the Bay is the Bay Area's annual hackathon run by students from Abraham Lincoln High School.

We want to encourage students from all backgrounds to come together, collaborate, and bring to life real world applications that create positive change. Our goal is to not only generate positive change in the community, but also to create an environment that brings all the necessary tools for creativity, networking and development in one place, providing several students with the opportunity to create an impact.
